Modes in Call of Duty: Black Ops III

Multiplayer

Multiplayer

Fast-paced, vertical combat with futuristic movement like wall-running and power slides. Choose from unique Specialists with insane abilities. Played across iconic maps like Combine and Hunted during the 2015 beta.(Activision, 2015)

Campaign

Campaign

Set in a tech-heavy 2065, you're a cyber-enhanced soldier diving into a mind-bending plot involving AI and rogue ops. Supports 4-player co-op and lets you hack, blast, and customize across open missions. (Polygon, 2015)

Zombies

Zombies

Zombies goes full horror-noir in Shadows of Evil, with twisted characters, Lovecraftian monsters, and deep lore. Beast Mode, GobbleGum, and epic maps like The Giant and Revelations make this mode legendary.(Treyarch, 2015)
